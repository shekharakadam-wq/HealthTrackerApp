🌅 Morning — 20 to 25 minutes
1
Seated deep breathing
Sit upright. Breathe in 4 counts, hold 2, out 6. Activates core, reduces shoulder tension.
5 min
2
Seated arm circles
Small slow circles — 15 forward, 15 backward. Warms up shoulder joints gently.
3 min
3
Shoulder blade squeezes ⭐
Squeeze blades together, hold 5 sec, release. 15 times. Most important — builds crutch-support muscles. Do this DAILY even if you skip everything else.
3 min — most important
4
Seated neck stretches
Tilt head side to side, hold 10 sec each. Chin to chest, hold 10 sec. Releases crutch tension.
2 min
5
Seated torso twists
Hands on shoulders, twist left and right slowly. 20 reps. Burns calories, works core muscles.
3 min
6
Seated forward punches 🔥
Punch alternately left-right at moderate pace. 3 sets of 30. Your cardio — raises heart rate, burns fat.
5 min — best fat burner
7
Wheelchair push intervals
Push wheelchair yourself. 30 sec fast, 30 sec rest, repeat 5 times. Great shoulder and arm workout.
5 min
🌙 Evening — 10 to 15 minutes
1
Resistance band pulls
Pull band apart in front of you, hold 3 sec, release. 3 sets of 12. Builds exact muscles for crutch use.
5 min
2
Lying leg raises
Raise left leg slowly 10–15 times. Lift right leg gently with hands. Improves circulation.
3 min
3
Shoulder self-massage
Warm mustard oil. Press and rub shoulder muscles with opposite hand. Rotate both wrists in circles.
5 min — do every night
📈 Week 1: just 15 min total. Add 5 min every 2 weeks. Within 6 weeks — real improvement in shoulder strength.
